What is MOTION IME

Motion Ime is a vibrant community created by popular Indonesian streamer Windah Basudara, along with his co-founder Rio Djaja. Initially established in 2023, the community started with a focus on 3D imaging merchandise like posters, stickers, and keychains, but it quickly expanded into a large and diverse online community on Discord. This platform, often called "Bocil Kematian" (a humorous term coined by Windah's fanbase), has grown to encompass various interests, including gaming, anime, cosplay, art, and more.

Why Choose Us

Diverse Interests and Activities

Motion Ime offers something for everyone, whether you're into gaming, anime, cosplay, art, or music. The community regularly hosts events like gaming parties (for games like Valorant and Minecraft), anime watch parties, and even creative discussions on photography, videography, and AI

Supportive Community

With a large, active Discord presence, you'll find a welcoming environment to connect with like-minded individuals. Motion Ime promotes positive interactions through its "Bocil Kematian" identity, which unites members in a lighthearted and inclusive atmosphere

Cultural Exchange

The community celebrates not just gaming, but also Japanese culture, anime, and cosplay. Joining Motion Ime gives you the chance to engage in cultural activities, whether you're attending virtual festivals or simply discussing the latest anime trends

Positive and Active Environment

Motion Ime fosters a positive and fun environment, offering spaces like "Free Talk" for casual conversations and support from fellow members. The community is lively and inclusive, with numerous forums and activities designed to keep members engaged and connected
